About Indra's Nectar

Indra's Nectar, known as Amrita in Sanskrit, is the divine drink of the gods in Hindu mythology. It is said to grant immortality and eternal youth to those who consume it.

According to ancient texts, this celestial elixir was created during the legendary Samudra Manthan - the churning of the cosmic ocean by gods and demons.

Our brand embodies the essence of this mythical nectar, bringing its divine qualities to the modern world.
